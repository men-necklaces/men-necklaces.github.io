Once you've chosen a style, the next decision is the gold itself. Understanding karats is crucial when selecting a solid gold necklace for men. The "k" refers to the purity of the gold, with 24k being pure gold.

A 14k gold necklace men is a fantastic all-around choice. It consists of 58.3% gold mixed with other alloys, making it highly durable and resistant to scratches. This makes it ideal for a piece you plan to wear often, like an everyday gold necklace men.

In contrast, an 18k gold necklace men contains 75% gold, giving it a richer, more vibrant yellow hue. While it's slightly softer than 14k gold due to its higher purity, it's prized for its luxurious color and premium feel, making it an excellent investment piece.
